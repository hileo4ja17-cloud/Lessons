The A2 formula

People

person + who + verb

A tour guide who speaks clearly is very helpful.

Things / jobs

thing/job + which (that) + verb

I want a part-time job which is creative.

Places

place + where + person + verb

This is the office where my team works.

Time

time/day/year + when + person + verb

Monday was the day when I felt encouraged.

Possession

person + whose + noun + verb

That is the writer whose book I love.

Extra note

which for things (that is also common in everyday English)

I like films which make me smile. (that make me smile)

2

Commas, only if you need them

A2 simple rule

Most of the time today, do not use commas.

I met a businessperson who works from home.

Use commas only when the information is like a small extra comment.

My sister, who is an architect, lives in London.
