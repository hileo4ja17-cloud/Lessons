2
Match each rule to the adjective that follows it.
Tap a rule (left), then tap its matching adjective (right).
rule
a) Adjectives with two syllables or more — use more / the most + the adjective.
b) One-syllable adjectives ending in one vowel + one consonant — double the last consonant and add -er.
c) Two-syllable adjectives ending in -y — drop the -y and add -ier.
d) One-syllable adjectives — just add -er.
e) Irregular adjectives — no set rule, you just have to learn them.

Bigger or smaller程度

modify your comparatives
6
Five little words that change how much.
Words go between is and the comparative: "Real life is slightly more complicated."
← big difference small difference →

big difference much

much a lot far

"Ordinary members of the public are much cheaper than actors."
"Korean fried chicken is far better than regular fried chicken." 🍗

small difference slightly

a bit slightly

"Real life is slightly more complicated."
"Season 2 of Demon Slayer is a bit more emotional than season 1."

↓ pick the modifier that fits
1. The new Spider-Man films are ______ more expensive than the old ones — like, 10× the budget.
slightly much a bit
2. Season 2 of Spy x Family is ______ funnier than season 1. Not by a huge amount, just a little.
far a bit a lot
3. The Glory is ______ darker than most romantic K-dramas. The difference is enormous.
slightly a bit far
4. My matcha latte is ______ sweeter than yours — I added half a teaspoon of honey, that's all.
slightly a lot much
↓ true or false? if false, fix the modifier
1. Jennifer Lawrence is slightly younger than Emilia Clarke. (They were born about a year apart.)
TRUE FALSE
✓ Correct! Jennifer was born in 1990, Emilia in 1986 — that's about 4 years, which is "slightly" or "a bit".
2. In the US, watching American football is much more popular than watching (European) football.
TRUE FALSE
✓ Correct! American football is far more popular than soccer in the United States.
3. Superman is a bit older than Spider-Man.
TRUE FALSE
FALSE → Superman first appeared in 1938 and Spider-Man in 1962 — that's a 24-year gap. So: "Superman is much / far older than Spider-Man."
4. Going to the cinema is far more expensive than renting films online.
TRUE FALSE
✓ Correct — cinema tickets cost several times more than streaming rentals.
5. Avengers: Endgame was far more expensive to make than Captain Marvel.
TRUE FALSE
✓ Correct! Endgame cost about $356 million, Captain Marvel about $175 million — roughly double.
