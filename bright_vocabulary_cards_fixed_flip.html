Bright Vocabulary Cards

Clickable vocabulary cards, audio for English words, and practice exercises. Click a card to flip it; click 🔊 to hear the word.

Cards: English → Russian

First you see the English word, then the translation and a short note.

Cards: Russian → English

First you see the translation, then the English word.

Exercises

Three tasks: matching, listening dictation, and gap fill.

1. Match the English word with the Russian meaning

Click a word on the left, then click the matching meaning on the right.

2. Listen and write

Click 🔊, listen to the word or phrase, and write it in English.

3. Fill in the missing words

Choose from the word bank and write the correct word or phrase in each sentence.
